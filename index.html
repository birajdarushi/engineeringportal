Civil Engineering

Design, construct, and maintain the built environment including buildings, roads, bridges, and infrastructure systems. Shape the future of sustainable urban development.

Mechanical Engineering

Design and manufacture mechanical systems, from tiny components to large machinery and vehicles. Drive innovation in robotics and automation.

Electrical Engineering

Work with electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism for power generation and electronic devices. Lead the future of renewable energy.

Computer Science

Design and develop computer systems, software applications, and digital solutions. Shape the future of technology and artificial intelligence.

Chemical Engineering

Design and operate chemical plants and processes for converting raw materials into valuable products. Pioneer sustainable solutions.

Aerospace Engineering

Design, develop, and produce aircraft and spacecraft systems for aviation and space exploration. Push the boundaries of human achievement.

Eligibility Requirements

Academic Requirements

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects
  • Minimum 60% aggregate in PCM subjects
  • Strong foundation in mathematics and analytical skills
  • Good communication and problem-solving abilities

Entrance Exams

  • JEE Main & Advanced - National level engineering entrance
  • State Engineering Entrance Exams - State-specific tests
  • BITSAT - Birla Institute of Technology entrance
  • VITEEE - VIT Engineering Entrance Examination
  • MET - Manipal Entrance Test

Additional Requirements

  • Age limit: Generally 17-25 years
  • Valid score card of qualifying examination
  • Domicile requirements (for state quotas)
  • Category certificates (if applicable)
